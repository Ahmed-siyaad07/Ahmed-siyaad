nasteexo waa mid ka mid xaafadaha ugu wanaagsan guud ahaan gobolka, gaar ahaan degamada wadajir.
aad bay u wanaagsantahay amni ahaan iyo deegaan ahaan marka loo eego xaafadaha keli.
